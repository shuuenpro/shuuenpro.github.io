New Fan's Guide

Shuuen no Shiori's Incompletion

On October 10th, 2015, Suzumu confessed to claiming ghostwritten songs as his own and earning dishonest profit from them, and announced that he would be retiring from all Vocaloid-related activities. (Translation here.)

The full extent of Suzumu's plagiarism is unknown. Additionally, because Suzumu had written/arranged songs outside of Shuuen no Shiori, we do not know if it extended to his involvement with the series.

Since the incident, both 150P and komine seem to have gone completely inactive without notice. Consequently, although the manga got its own original ending in January of 2016, the project itself has never received a proper conclusion (and more than likely never will).

You are welcome to get into the series in spite of this, but be aware that there are countless mysteries unsolved and many loose ends untied.
