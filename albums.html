    A doujin CD that acts sort of like a one-shot (i.e. first draft) album for Shuuen. Includes the original IA versions of the first four songs (except NND / final version of D-ne's song) and 2 new songs.

  • Shuuen -Re:write- (終焉-Re:write-)

    (Released 2013/03/13) (2 discs, 27 + 5 tracks)

    First major album of Shuuen! Focuses on the occult club. Includes new unique Vocaloid covers of the occult club's songs plus Underdog Supremacy, Common Heroes, and a remaster of Bookmark of Demise. Includes 4 new songs.
