A Guide to Shuuen no Shiori

by @ALTCODE255

Introduction

The Bookmark of Demise Project (終焉ノ栞プロジェクト, Shuuen no Shiori Purojekuto) (May 12th, 2012 – November 15th, 2015), colloquially known as Shuuenpro, is a multimedia horror-mystery Vocaloid series created by

  • 150P (music)
  • Suzumu (スズム) (story & lyrics)
  • komine (こみね) (illustration)
  • Saine (さいね) (illustration & character design)

represented in the form of music, light novels, and manga. Its story contains major elements of gore, death, and unreality (+ other content warnings).

It is important to note that Shuuen no Shiori is UNFINISHED, and is highly unlikely to ever get an ending.

This website is meant to serve as an actively maintained resource for both new and existing fans of the series. It contains up-to-date (translation) masterlists on Shuuenpro's:

  • Albums / Songs
  • Light Novels
  • Manga
  • Album Crossfades & Song MVs

If you're a new fan looking to get into Shuuen no Shiori, click here to proceed to the New Fan's Guide.

Novel 1 Prologue (Preview)

The beginning of the story. The opening act.

Outside of the window was a cloudy sky. The season in which summer has not yet started. There existed a single rumor whispered about.
No one knows the details. No, no one was supposed to know them.
Just that, even if the empty book and bookmark are found, it is said that they must never be touched.

—For it is the Bookmark of Demise.

On the other side of the unsolved case, the rumor-loving high school students became involved in a certain ritual. From the extremely popular songs on Nico Nico Douga, begins the Project.
