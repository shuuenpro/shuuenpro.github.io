Introduction

The Bookmark of Demise Project (終焉ノ栞プロジェクト, Shuuen no Shiori Purojekuto) (May 12th, 2012 – January 27th, 2016), colloquially known as Shuuenpro, is a multimedia horror-mystery (+ metafictional?) Vocaloid series created by

  • 150P (music)
  • Suzumu (スズム) (story & lyrics)
  • komine (こみね) (illustration)
  • Saine (さいね) (illustration & character design)

represented in the form of music, light novels, and manga. Common content warnings for the series include gore, death, and unreality (see more here).

It is important to note that Shuuen no Shiori is UNFINISHED, and is highly unlikely to ever get an ending.

If you're a new fan looking to get into Shuuen no Shiori, click here to proceed to the New Fan's Guide.

Novel 1 Prologue (Preview)

The beginning of the story. The opening act.

Outside of the window was a cloudy sky. The season in which summer has not yet started. There existed a single rumor whispered about.
No one knows the details. No, no one was supposed to know them.
Just that, even if the empty book and bookmark are found, it is said that they must never be touched.

—For it is the Bookmark of Demise.
