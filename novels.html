Light Novels

Shuuen no Shiori's light novel adaptation was written by Suzumu, and illustrated by komine (black-and-white illustrations) and Saine (cover art, colored illustrations). Altogether, there exist 4 light novels.

According to the 4th novel's afterword, a 5th light novel, named "Bookmark of Demise 5: Conclusion Dreamelody", was planned (but never released).
